Experience

I served for PLKN for 2 months in Cherating, Pahang. It was an astonishing experience eventhough it was only for 2 months. In PLKN, I am able to learn a lot of new skill and knowledge. There was a time when I got the experience to practise to fire using rifle. Other than that, I also got to experience to jungle camping which we required to find a camping spot in the jungle that my team had to walk over 20km to set up our camp. I have never been on a war ship before and now I know how big is was and the technologies on the ship is very fascinating. Next, I considered myself as luckt becuase i had the chance to fly a jet using a high technology jet simulator which it looks so real with a real map. I cant never forget all the memories that i had during PLKN.
